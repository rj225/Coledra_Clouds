    About Disaster Recovery of Cloud technologies

    Building a solidly tiered Disaster Recovery plan is a big challenge in itself. But then testing it is a nightmare for business continuity – it can be painfully disruptive and prohibitive. An ideal Disaster Recovery solution should be simple and effective (not to mention, with lightning fast RTOs and RPOs).

    Disaster recovery is a critical aspect of any business continuity strategy. In today's interconnected world, organizations need to be prepared for unforeseen events that can disrupt their operations. Whether it's a natural disaster like a hurricane or earthquake, a cyber attack that compromises data security, or a hardware failure that leads to system downtime, the impact on businesses can be devastating.

    Virtual Machine Recovery

    Our virtual machine recovery services enable rapid restoration of your entire IT infrastructure in a virtualized environment. In the event of a disaster, your systems can be quickly spun up on virtual machines, minimizing downtime and ensuring business continuity.

    Feature Description
    Instant Recovery Quickly recover virtual machines with minimal downtime.
    Granular Recovery Selectively restore specific files or applications within virtual machines.
    Automated Failover Automatically failover to virtual machines in case of a primary system failure.
